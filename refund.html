Booking, Payment & Refund Policy

Advance Payment

  • A 50% advance payment of the total booking amount is mandatory at the time of booking to confirm your trip.

  • The remaining balance must be paid as per the agreed terms before or during the journey.

Cancellation & Refund Policy

    Cancellation 2 days prior to the booked travel date:

  • A full refund will be processed.

  • GST amount will be deducted, as GST is non-refundable.

    • Cancellation 1 day prior or on the same day of travel:

  • 50% of the advance payment will be deducted.

  • The remaining amount (if applicable) will be refunded as per policy.

  • No refund will be applicable for trips cancelled after the journey has started or in case of no-show.

GST Clause

  • GST charged at the time of booking is non-refundable under any circumstances, as per government regulations.
