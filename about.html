About us page

Welcome to Travel with Chhonkars — your trusted travel partner with over 20 years of experience in the travel industry. Since 2006, we have been proudly serving travelers with smooth, reliable, and comfortable travel experiences designed to meet individual needs. Located at A-8, Om Nagar, Natvar Nagar, Dholi Pyau, Mathura, our company is owned and managed by Mr. Geetam Singh, who has built this business with dedication, honesty, and customer satisfaction at its core. At Travel with Chhonkars, we specialize in Mathura to anywhere in India taxi and tour services. Whether you're looking for a spiritual journey, family vacation, or business trip, we ensure every ride is safe, enjoyable, and stress-free.
